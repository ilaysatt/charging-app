How to use this app
  • Track EV charging costs per vehicle (by VIN)
  • Log charges by entering battery start/end % or direct kWh
  • Set your electricity price and kWh per 1% at the top
  • Filter history by VIN or month
  • Go to Reports to generate per-VIN summaries by date range
  • Export reports as PDF or download all data as CSV
